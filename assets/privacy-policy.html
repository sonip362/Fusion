Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our login system, and participate in our loyalty program.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide a premium shopping experience. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, or similar identifier provided during MongoDB registration.
  • Contact Data: Email address for order confirmations and account security.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including the items you add to your cart and the coupons you unlock.
  • Loyalty Data: Your "Fusion Credits" and point balance.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only as necessary to run the Fusion engine:

  • To Manage Your Account: Using MongoDB to ensure your cart and points are accessible across all your devices.
  • To Personalize Experience: Our AI-driven logic uses your preferences to suggest the right "Royal Indian Studios" silk sarees or "Fusion" techwear.
  • To Process Rewards: To calculate and apply tiered discounts.
  • Security: To prevent "JavaScript tampering" and ensure loyalty points are earned legitimately.

3. Storage & Cookies

  • MongoDB Atlas: Your profile and points are stored securely in a cloud-based MongoDB database. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.
  • Local Storage: We may use small files on your device to remember your session and ensure the Preloader doesn't delay your experience unnecessarily.
  • Third-Party: We do not sell your data to any third-party advertisers.

4. Your Rights

As a user of Fusion, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you in our database.
  • Correction: Ask us to fix any incorrect information (e.g., a typo in your name).
  • Deletion: Request that we "wipe" your MongoDB profile and points balance.
  • We use minimal cookies to ensure the "Sliding Cart" and "Login Drawer" function correctly. By using our site, you consent to these functional cookies which are essential for UI to operate.
  • If you have questions about this policy or how your "Fusion Credits" are managed, please contact the Fusion Development Team.
